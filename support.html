FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. There are no accounts and no sign-up. Just open the app and start.

Does it work offline?

Yes — transcription runs on your iPhone by default, no internet required.

How do I get the most accurate Ukrainian (or best quality)?

In Settings, add your own free Groq API key. Think Aloud then transcribes via Whisper large-v3 — far more accurate than built-in recognition, especially for Ukrainian. You can also use OpenAI, or Anthropic (Claude) for text cleanup.

Is my data private?

Yes. Recordings and transcripts stay on your device. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I export my notes?

Open the tree, select conversations, and export them as one Markdown document (raw or structured) to Notes, Obsidian, Telegram, etc.
